About The Kingdom

"Kingdom" is an acclaimed video game series created by the talented developer Thomas van den Berg, known as "noio," and published by Raw Fury. This captivating series consists of titles like "Kingdom: Classic," "Kingdom: New Lands," and "Kingdom: Two Crowns," each offering a unique blend of resource management, exploration, and strategic tower defense. In "Kingdom," players assume the role of a Monarch tasked with building, expanding, and defending their kingdom from the mysterious Greed, while embracing a visually striking minimalist pixel art style. Thomas van den Berg's brilliant artistry and Raw Fury's publishing expertise have combined to create an unforgettable gaming experience, making the "Kingdom" series a beloved staple in the realm of indie games.

The Lore

In the mystical land of Cedaria, there existed six ancient Kingdoms, each with its unique history, culture, and people. These Kingdoms stood as bastions of prosperity and power, their rule unchallenged for generations.
